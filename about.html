Sharan Musician

Sharan S S, professionally known as Sharan Musician, is an Indian music composer, producer, and playback singer whose work stands at the intersection of cinematic storytelling, classical depth, and modern sound design. Known for his emotionally driven compositions and refined production sensibility, Sharan represents a new generation of musicians shaping contemporary Indian music with global intent and discipline.

Born and raised in Marthandam, Kanyakumari District, Tamil Nadu, Sharan’s journey into music began at the age of six. What started as early musical curiosity evolved into a lifelong commitment to sound, structure, and emotional expression. From a young age, he demonstrated a natural understanding of melody and rhythm, gradually building proficiency across multiple instruments including piano, violin, and drums—skills that later became central to his compositional identity.

EDUCATION

LCM University of West London (Music Theory & Performance)

ENGINEERING

B.E. Mechanical Engineering, Anna University

His professional career formally began with the release of his first independent single, “Um Anbinal”, on 24 February 2021. Sharan made his official composer debut for the Tamil short film “Hunter” on 14 May 2023, and further expanded his role with the Tamil short film “IPC-100” in 2024.

Musical Identity

Artistically, Sharan’s work is defined by melody-first storytelling, layered arrangements, and a cinematic sense of scale. His music blends Indian classical influences, cinematic orchestration, and contemporary styles including EDM, Hip-Hop, and Regional Rhythms.

Notable releases such as Manadu, Kannadi Kaadhal, On Air, Broken Wings, Heart of a Loser, and Enna Naan Seiven Unnodu Sera showcase his ability to move seamlessly between genres while maintaining a consistent emotional core. Today, he continues to collaborate with global creators, making his work accessible on all major streaming services.
